    • Who we are

    Southern Can Making Company Limited (SCMC) is a fully owned Saudi company, and its manufacturing facility is located in Al Shumaissi area, 50 km away from Jeddah city. It is the canmanufacturing facility of Alesayi Holding Company (AHC) specializing in producing beverage cans and can-ends.

    Its main product in its can production lines are 2-piece steel and aluminum cans of sizes 250ml, 330ml, and 355ml, whereas in its can-end production line are 200 and 202 diameter SOT and SRP.

    SCMC had already established itself as one of the major companies in the Middle East region for manufacturing 2-piece beverage cans and can ends. It is an ISO certified manufacturing plant (9001:2008 and 22000:2005) for Quality Management System and Food Safety Management System respectively. Putting its highest value in providing quality cans and can-ends, it upgraded its quality and food safety standards to meet the latest ISO certification requirements.

    In addition, it also acquired the state-of-the art sets of equipment for quality testing and monitoring to ensure that all of its products does not only meet its customer’s requirements but also exceeds their expectations.
