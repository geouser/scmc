About Us

Southern Can Making Company Limited (SCMC) is a fully owned Saudi company, and its manufacturing facility is located in Al Shumaissi area, 50 km away from Jeddah city. It is the can manufacturing facility of Alesayi Holding Company (AHC) specializing in producing beverage cans and can-ends.
